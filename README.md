# Mechanical Design Projects (Coursework)

Two undergraduate course projects from Mechanical Manufacturing I & II 
at Hankyong National University (2025).

## 1. Optimal Design of Railway Rail Web Section
**File**: `rail_web_optimization.pdf`

Multi-objective optimization (cooling efficiency vs. critical buckling 
load vs. mass) of railway rail web cross-section under forced 
convection cooling. Used correlation matrix analysis and Response 
Surface Optimization in ANSYS Workbench.

**Key methods**: Transient Thermal Analysis, Eigenvalue Buckling, 
Response Surface Method, Multi-objective optimization

## 2. Stress Concentration Mitigation in Double-Nut Systems
**File**: `double_nut_stress.pdf`

Investigation of taper angle effect on clamping force and stress 
concentration in HARDLOCK-type wedge nuts (HLN M12). Compared 
analytical static equilibrium model (MATLAB) with FEA (ANSYS).

**Key methods**: Static equilibrium analysis, FEA, Theory-simulation 
comparison

## Note

These are coursework projects (not peer-reviewed publications), 
but they represent my interest in design optimization and 
theory-FEA validation methodologies.
